Disaster Readiness

Hurricanes

1.Create a hurricane evacuation plan with your family.

2. Stock up on non-perishable food, water, and medications.

3.Board up windows and secure outdoor furniture.

4. Stay informed about weather conditions through reliable news sources.

5. Consider installing storm shutters or impact-resistant windows.

Earthquakes

1. Secure heavy furniture and objects to walls to prevent them from falling.

2. Store emergency supplies, such as food, water, and first aid kits.

3. Practice earthquake drills with your family.

4. Know evacuation routes and emergency shelters.

5. Consider installing earthquake-resistant fasteners on heavy objects.

Floods

1. Know your flood risk and evacuation routes.

2. Store valuable items and important documents in a safe place.

3. Keep emergency supplies, such as food, water, and first aid kits.

4. Consider installing flood-proof barriers or sandbags.

5. Stay informed about weather conditions through reliable news sources.

Wildfires

1. Create a wildfire evacuation plan with your family.

2. Keep emergency supplies, such as food, water, and first aid kits.

3. Clear flammable vegetation and debris from around your home.

4. Stay informed about fire conditions through reliable news sources.

5. Consider installing fire-resistant roofing and siding.
